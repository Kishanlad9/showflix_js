Overview

Kantara 2 is a prequel story centered on the conflict between a tyrannical king and the people of the Kantara forest, focusing on the origins of the Panjurli and Guliga Daivas. The narrative is set before the events of the original film, in pre-colonial Karnataka, and follows the character Berme (played by Rishab Shetty) as he defends his people against the king and a rival clan that uses black magic to try and capture their deities. The story culminates in Berme embracing divine power, a climactic battle, and a finale that sets up the possibility of a third film.
